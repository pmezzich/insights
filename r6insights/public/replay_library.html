How to find your .rec files

Replay files are stored locally on your PC by Rainbow Six Siege — they are not uploaded to R6 Insights. Use the match metadata below to identify the correct file, then locate it at this path on your machine:

Windows: C:\Users\[YourName]\Documents\My Games\Rainbow Six - Siege\[ProfileID]\Match Replays\
Ubisoft Connect: Open Ubisoft Connect → Rainbow Six Siege → Play → Replays
Files are named by match ID and date. Match them using the date, opponent, and maps shown below. Note: Siege only keeps the last ~30 replays before overwriting old ones.
