Finding your .rec files

Replay files are stored locally by Siege — not uploaded here. Use the match data below to find the matching file on your PC.

Windows: C:\Users\[Name]\Documents\My Games\Rainbow Six - Siege\[ProfileID]\Match Replays\
Ubisoft Connect: Open → Rainbow Six Siege → Play → Replays

Siege keeps the last ~30 replays. Match by date, opponent and map.
